## 📊 Data Verification Standards

### Our Sources

All claims in this repository are sourced from:

**Tier 1 (Highest reliability)**:
- ACLED (Armed Conflict Location & Event Data)
- International Crisis Group
- Council on Foreign Relations (CFR)
- UN agencies (UNHCR, OCHA)
- Peer-reviewed academic journals

**Tier 2 (Verified reporting)**:
- BBC, Reuters, AP, AFP
- Regional specialist media (Bangkok Post, Al Jazeera, etc.)

**Tier 3 (Contextual/supplementary)**:
- Local NGOs
- Social media (verified accounts only)
- Government statements (clearly labeled as such)

### Cross-Verification

Every major claim has **minimum 2 independent sources**. When single-sourced, it's explicitly noted.

### Updates & Corrections

We issue corrections via:
- Commit messages (GitHub history is public)
- Updated "Last Modified" timestamps on each file
- Issues thread for major corrections

## 📝 Sample Story Frameworks

### Breaking News Template

1. **Lede**: Current status (casualties, displacement, escalation)
2. **Context**: Historical background (from our case study timelines)
3. **Regional impact**: Geopolitical implications (from systemic risk analysis)
4. **Human cost**: On-ground reporting (connect with our NGO contacts)
5. **What's next**: Scenario modeling (from Volume VI)

### Investigative Template

1. **Hook**: What's been overlooked?
2. **The pattern**: Connect dots across our datasets
3. **Follow the money/power**: Use network analysis
4. **Voices**: Experts + affected populations
5. **Accountability**: Who benefits? Who pays?
