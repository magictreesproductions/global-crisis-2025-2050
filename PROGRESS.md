## 🎯 Project Overview

This repository provides comprehensive, OSINT-verified analysis of interconnected global crises spanning 2025-2050. Content is specifically designed for journalists, researchers, educators, and policymakers seeking factual, well-sourced information about complex geopolitical, environmental, and institutional challenges.

**Mission**: Make critical global crisis data accessible, verifiable, and usable for investigative journalism and public accountability.
