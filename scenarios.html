12 High-Leverage Intervention Points

These intervention points represent areas where targeted actions can create cascading positive effects across multiple crisis domains, potentially shifting the probability distribution toward more favorable outcomes.

Algorithm Regulation

Change social media metrics from "engagement" to "veracity" to reduce information warfare and polarization.

Resource Traceability

Blockchain tracking of conflict minerals and resources to reduce funding for proxy warfare.

Quantum Cryptography

Global migration to post-quantum cryptographic standards before "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" attacks.

Climate Engineering

Large-scale interventions in Earth's climate system to prevent catastrophic warming and migration.

UBI Implementation

Universal Basic Income reducing economic insecurity and authoritarian susceptibility.

Media Reform

Supporting diverse, independent information sources to combat disinformation and manipulation.

International Cooperation

Strengthening global governance institutions to address transnational challenges effectively.

Technology Regulation

Frameworks for responsible AI and biotechnology development to prevent existential risks.

Renewable Energy

Rapid transition to clean energy systems to reduce resource conflicts and climate impacts.

Circular Economy

Transforming production and consumption patterns to achieve sustainability and reduce waste.

Education Reform

Building 21st-century skills and critical thinking to create informed, engaged citizens.

Democratic Renewal

Strengthening participatory governance institutions to maintain legitimacy and effectiveness.
